Follow these simple steps to make a loan payment through LoanSphere:

  1. Log in to your LoanSphere account.
  2. Go to the "My Loans" section from the dashboard.
  3. Select the loan you want to make a payment for.
  4. Click on "Make a Payment".
  5. Choose your preferred payment method:
    • Credit/Debit Card
    • UPI
    • Net Banking
    • Wallet (Paytm/PhonePe etc.)
  6. Enter the amount and confirm the transaction.
  7. You will receive a confirmation email and SMS once the payment is successful.
